ARM WORKOUT

Build strong and defined arms with these powerful exercises

5
Exercises
~16
Minutes
💪
Power
👊
Punches
Duration: 30 seconds
Target: Arms and shoulders
Tip: Keep your core tight and punch with control
⏱️
Rest Break
Duration: 5 seconds
Action: Shake out your arms and prepare
💎
Diamond Push-Ups
Reps: 20 repetitions
Target: Triceps and chest
Tip: Form a diamond shape with your hands
⏱️
Rest Break
Duration: 5 seconds
Action: Take deep breaths
🧱
Wall Push-Ups
Reps: 16 repetitions
Target: Arms and chest
Tip: Stand arm's length from the wall
⏱️
Rest Break
Duration: 5 seconds
Action: Prepare for bicep curls
🏋️
Leg Barbell Curl Left
Reps: 16 repetitions
Target: Left bicep
Tip: Control the movement, don't swing
⏱️
Rest Break
Duration: 5 seconds
Action: Switch to right arm
🏋️
Leg Barbell Curl Right
Reps: 16 repetitions
Target: Right bicep
Tip: Maintain proper form throughout
